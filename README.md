# Sprout & Soil

A tiny free web gardening game made with plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## How to play

Open `index.html` in a browser.

- Plant seeds in empty soil.
- Pick between carrot, tomato, and sunflower seeds.
- Water planted patches once per day.
- Click `Next Day` to grow watered plants.
- Pay 2 coins in tax every 7 days.
- Harvest mature plants for coins and more seeds.
- Buy seed packs when you need more seeds.
- Buy more land for 1000 coins to unlock 4 extra plots.
- Click `Spray Game` to play a timed worm-spraying minigame for bonus coins.
- Your progress saves automatically in the browser.

## What to change next

- Add different plant types with different prices.
- Add pests, weather, decorations, or quests.
- Add music, sound effects, and animations.
